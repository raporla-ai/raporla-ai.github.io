About RAPORLA

Raporla is an AI-driven solution designed to facilitate the reporting process for doctors. The system utilizes speech-to-text technology fine-tuned specifically for Turkish doctors, automating and streamlining the report creation process. Also the system will assist doctors in preliminary diagnosis through checking medical database articles and patient reports that are relevant to the generated report.
